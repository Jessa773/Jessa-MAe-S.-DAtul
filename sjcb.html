MISSION


  1. Spirituality

    2. To be witness of our faith by cultivating a spirituality rooted on the Word of God,nourished by the sacraments, and lived in humble loving service especially to the poor.

  2. Transformative Education

    3. To provide Catholic Education leading to a conversion that transforms persons and communities.

  3. Advocacy

    4. To uphold the principles of social justice, peace,truth and ecological stewardship towards an engaged citizenship.

  4. Research

    5. To foster a vibrant and stimulating culture of research.

  5. Synodality

    6. To promote a culture of care dialogue, and collaboration among all the stakeholders as a dynamic participation in the life and mission of the church
